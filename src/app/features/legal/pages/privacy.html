Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

At UniDelivery, your privacy is our priority. We collect personal data explicitly provided by you when creating an account, including your full name, phone number, and ID credentials for courier verification.

To facilitate accurate logistics, we continuously process your live GPS coordinates while you are marked as actively delivering a package or searching for couriers.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Service Execution: Matching Sender requests with the nearest available Courier.
  • Security: Verifying courier identities to prevent fraud and ensure package safety.
  • Financial Processing: Facilitating payments and withdrawals via your integrated UniCoins wallet through compliant third-party gateways (Stripe/Clover).

3. Data Sharing and Protection

We do NOT sell your personal data to third parties. Information is only shared symmetrically (e.g., sharing your current live location with the Sender of the package you are holding) for the duration of the delivery objective.

All traffic is secured via end-to-end HTTPS encryption, and sensitive data like ID photos are stored securely on our enterprise blob networks.
